Our Story

SPRINT is a national level sporting fest organised by SPJIMR, Mumbai. SPRINT is in its eleventh year now, having quickly captured the passion of MBA students across India. SPRINT is organised in the month of January, and sees participation of more than 25 B-schools with 2500+ participants and 5000+ footfalls. SPRINT is the manifestation of the burning desire of proving oneself, keeping the sportsman spirit flying high and epitomises what management is all about- team work! SPRINT brings to students, a great opportunity for self-expression and honing sporting talent. Networking with the best minds of India through sport is a happy consequence.
