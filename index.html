Our Story

SPRINT is the annual sports festival of SPJIMR, Mumbai. SPRINT 2018 is truly a celebration of courage and heart @SPJIMR! Scheduled on the 12th, 13th and 14th of Jan’18, it is a platform for all the _Sher-Dils_ out there to bring out the Warrior inside, prove their mettle on the battlefield and ultimately emerge as a Conqueror (of kingdoms and hearts)! So charge up for the adrenaline-rush experience at the beginning of the new year 2018!
